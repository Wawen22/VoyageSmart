## 👤 Step 2: Create Your Account

### Registration Options

#### Option A: Email Registration

1. **Click "Get Started"** on the landing page
2. **Fill in the registration form**:
   ```
   Email: your-email@example.com
   Password: ••••••••••••
   Confirm Password: ••••••••••••
   Full Name: Your Full Name
   ```
   - **Password**: Choose a strong password (8+ characters)
   - **Full Name**: Your display name
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for a verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

#### Option B: Social Login (if configured)

1. Click **"Sign in with Google"** or **"Sign in with GitHub"**
2. Authorize VoyageSmart to access your account
3. Complete any additional profile information

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After signing in for the first time:

1. You'll be redirected to the **Profile Setup** page
2. Add or update your information:
   - **Profile Picture**: Upload an avatar (optional)
   - **Full Name**: Confirm or update your name
   - **Preferences**: Set your default currency, timezone, etc.
3. Click **"Save Profile"**

## ✈️ Creating Your First Trip

### Step 1: Start a New Trip

1. Click **"Create Trip"** in the navigation or dashboard
2. You'll see the **New Trip** form

### Step 2: Fill in Trip Details

Complete the following information:

#### Basic Information
- **Trip Name**: Give your trip a memorable name (e.g., "Summer Vacation 2024")
- **Destination**: Where you're going (e.g., "Paris, France")
- **Description**: Brief description of your trip (optional)

#### Dates
- **Start Date**: When your trip begins
- **End Date**: When your trip ends
- **Timezone**: The timezone of your destination

#### Trip Settings
- **Visibility**: 
  - **Private**: Only you can see the trip
  - **Shared**: Invited participants can view and edit
- **Currency**: Default currency for expenses
- **Budget**: Optional overall trip budget

### Step 3: Create the Trip

1. Review your information
2. Click **"Create Trip"**
3. You'll be redirected to your new trip's dashboard

## 👥 Adding Participants

### Inviting Others

1. In your trip dashboard, click **"Manage Participants"**
2. Click **"Invite Participant"**
3. Enter the participant's email address
4. Choose their role:
   - **Admin**: Can edit all trip details and invite others
   - **Participant**: Can view and add to the trip
   - **Viewer**: Can only view trip information
5. Add a personal message (optional)
6. Click **"Send Invitation"**

### Managing Participants

- **View Participants**: See all current trip members
- **Change Roles**: Update participant permissions
- **Remove Participants**: Remove someone from the trip
- **Resend Invitations**: Send invitation reminders

## 📅 Planning Your Itinerary

### Adding Your First Activity

1. Go to the **"Itinerary"** tab in your trip
2. Click **"Add Activity"**
3. Fill in the activity details:
   - **Activity Name**: What you're doing
   - **Date**: When it's happening
   - **Time**: Start and end times
   - **Location**: Where it's taking place
   - **Notes**: Additional details
4. Click **"Save Activity"**

### Itinerary Views

VoyageSmart offers multiple ways to view your itinerary:

- **List View**: Simple chronological list
- **Calendar View**: Traditional calendar layout
- **Map View**: See activities on an interactive map

### Using the AI Wizard (if enabled)

1. Click **"AI Wizard"** in the itinerary section
2. Answer the wizard's questions about:
   - Your interests and preferences
   - Budget considerations
   - Activity types you enjoy
3. Review the AI-generated suggestions
4. Add activities you like to your itinerary

## 💰 Tracking Expenses

### Adding Your First Expense

1. Go to the **"Expenses"** tab in your trip
2. Click **"Add Expense"**
3. Fill in the expense details:
   - **Description**: What you spent money on
   - **Amount**: How much you spent
   - **Currency**: The currency used
   - **Category**: Type of expense (food, transport, etc.)
   - **Date**: When the expense occurred
   - **Paid By**: Who paid for this expense
4. Click **"Save Expense"**

### Splitting Expenses

1. When adding an expense, check **"Split this expense"**
2. Select which participants should share the cost
3. Choose how to split:
   - **Equally**: Split evenly among selected participants
   - **Custom**: Set specific amounts for each person
4. Save the expense

### Expense Reports

View your spending with:
- **Category Breakdown**: See spending by category
- **Participant Summary**: Who owes what to whom
- **Daily Spending**: Track daily expenses
- **Budget Progress**: Compare against your budget

## 🏨 Managing Accommodations

### Adding Accommodations

1. Go to the **"Accommodations"** tab
2. Click **"Add Accommodation"**
3. Enter details:
   - **Name**: Hotel/accommodation name
   - **Type**: Hotel, Airbnb, hostel, etc.
   - **Check-in/Check-out**: Dates and times
   - **Address**: Location details
   - **Booking Reference**: Confirmation number
   - **Cost**: Total cost of accommodation
4. Save the accommodation

## 🚗 Managing Transportation

### Adding Transportation

1. Go to the **"Transportation"** tab
2. Click **"Add Transportation"**
3. Choose transportation type:
   - **Flight**: Airline travel
   - **Train**: Rail travel
   - **Car Rental**: Vehicle rental
   - **Other**: Bus, taxi, etc.
4. Fill in relevant details:
   - **From/To**: Departure and arrival locations
   - **Date/Time**: Travel schedule
   - **Booking Reference**: Confirmation details
   - **Cost**: Transportation cost
5. Save the transportation

### AI-Powered Suggestions

Throughout the app, you'll see AI suggestions for:
- **Activities**: Based on your destination and interests
- **Restaurants**: Local dining recommendations
- **Transportation**: Optimal routes and methods
- **Packing**: What to bring based on weather and activities

### Common Questions

**Q: Can I use VoyageSmart offline?**
A: Some features work offline, but you'll need internet for real-time sync and AI features.

**Q: How many trips can I create?**
A: Free users can create unlimited trips with basic features. Premium users get advanced features.

**Q: Can I export my trip data?**
A: Yes, you can export trip information as PDF or CSV from the trip settings.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes, VoyageSmart uses enterprise-grade security with encrypted data storage.
